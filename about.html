TANYA HICKS
“I do not choose between soul and science, body and ethics, pleasure and responsibility. I work at the point where those false divides collapse.”
Forty-seven was a big year. I am a late-diagnosed AuDHD counsellor, solo parent, and author — with PMDD and hypermobile Ehlers-Danlos layered underneath everything I once called coping. For most of my life I could hyper-focus on skill development with surgical precision. And I was exhausted in ways none of it ever explained.
