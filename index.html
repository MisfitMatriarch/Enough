Once you can see what it is costing you, the next question is why.

Not all of it comes from the same place. Some comes from your wiring. Some from the conditions around you. Some from what life has required you to carry for a long time.

The Capacity Map moves through nine layers — from neurology to current state — so you can see which layers are bearing the most weight right now.

What this helps you see

  • Which layers are shaping your capacity and where the account is actually being drawn from.
  • Capacity is shaped by architecture, not just effort.

Some things change with effort. Some shift with the right support. Some are structural limits that effort alone will never move.

That distinction matters more than most people realise. A significant part of what exhausts capable people is spending what they have pushing against things that were never theirs to move alone.

What this helps you see

  • What sits in your control, what sits in your influence, and what sits beyond both.
  • Where to stop spending, and where change is actually possible.
