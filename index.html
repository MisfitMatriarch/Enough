The Framework

The shift is from capacity-building to condition-building

Tanya helps practitioners and organisations ask a different question - not only “what can this person do?”, but “what conditions are shaping what we’re able to see?”

Capacity is conditional

The same person can look capable in one setting and unable in another. That is data about conditions, not character.

Behaviour can be protection

What looks like resistance, avoidance, shutdown, or inconsistency may be survival, adaptation, or nervous-system defence - not a deficit.

Support should build conditions

Support is not the destination. It should create the conditions where sovereignty becomes possible.
