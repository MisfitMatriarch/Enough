Keynotes & Presentations
Tanya’s presentations combine lived experience, clinical insight, and systems thinking to help audiences understand why life can quietly cost some people far more energy than it appears to. Her work resonates with: • Leadership and executive audiences • Health and mental health professionals • Educators and education leaders • Disability and inclusion sectors • Community and social impact organisations • Women’s leadership and entrepreneurship forums Each presentation focuses on clarity, dignity, and practical insight — not motivation or productivity pressure.
