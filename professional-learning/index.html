From orientation to implementation

  • Keynote or 90-minute foundation session
  • Half-day or full-day practice lab
  • Assessment, formulation and plan-quality clinic
  • Multi-session applied professional-learning programme
  • Reflective-practice consultation
  • Case consultation
  • Supervisor development for people already holding appropriate supervisory roles
  • Community of practice
  • Organisational implementation and evidence audit

Scope note: reflective-practice and case consultation are not represented as accredited clinical supervision. Formal counselling, clinical or Behaviour Support supervision is offered only when the relevant supervisor recognition, practitioner capability, insurance and governance requirements are met. Completion of a course does not certify competence.
