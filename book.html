What This Book Is
The exhaustion that never made sense. Not the tired that sleep fixes. The tired that quietly takes your patience, your creativity, your focus, and your hope. ENOUGH shows you why it’s happening — and what becomes possible when it stops.
What You Will Not Find Here
— Pressure to produce more — A ranking of who is doing life correctly — A demand for constant growth — A dismissal of disability or constraint — A promise that systems will suddenly become fair
What You Will Find
→ Clarity → Limits that are acknowledged → An explanation that actually fits → A different place to put your energy The direction remains yours. Always.
