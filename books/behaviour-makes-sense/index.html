Evidence & scope

This is a professional practice text. It sets out method, evidence expectations, scope and implementation. It does not certify competence, replace supervision, or authorise any practice beyond a practitioner’s qualifications, registration and governance.

In practice at Neurodivergent Empowered

Specialist Behaviour Support participant orientation, team education and professional partnerships.

Accessible formats planned: print, ebook, audio, Easy Read or visual summary, large print, chapter sampler. No book is required to receive support, and complimentary copies are never claimed from an NDIS plan.
