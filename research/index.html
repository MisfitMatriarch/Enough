Access Before Intervention: Developing an Evidence-Informed Nervous System Nourishment Practice Guide for Neurodivergent-Affirming Counselling and Behaviour Support Practice

A supervised capstone and practice-development project. It is not a treatment-validation trial, and not evidence that Nervous System Nourishment is already a validated intervention. It is a critical development of pre-existing work rather than its unexamined reproduction.
